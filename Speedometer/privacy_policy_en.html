Privacy Policy

Last Updated: Nov 16, 2025

1. Introduction

We prioritize privacy. Core data is processed on-device whenever possible.

2. Information

  • Location & Speed (Core Location) for real-time display; not for profiling or tracking.
  • Motion & Orientation (Core Motion: accelerometer, gyroscope, magnetometer).
  • Bluetooth for optional tests; no personal Bluetooth data collection.
  • Camera/Microphone for optional tests; no background recording/uploading.
  • Anonymous usage and crash logs for stability improvements.

3. Subscriptions & Ads

  • StoreKit 2 handles payments; we do not access payment details.
  • AdMob in the free version; with ATT consent, device identifiers may be used for personalization and frequency capping.

4. Storage & Security

  • History/reports stored on-device by default; optional iCloud sync by Apple.
  • No server-side personal profiles; best-practice security.

5. Sharing

No selling of personal data. Sharing only when necessary, required by law, or to protect rights.

6. Your Rights

  • Access/Correct/Delete local data.
  • Withdraw location/tracking consent; disable ad personalization.

7. Children

No intentional collection from children under 13.
