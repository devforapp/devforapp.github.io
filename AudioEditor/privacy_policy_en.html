Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 20, 2024

Privacy First: AudioEditor is designed with privacy in mind. All audio processing is performed locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your audio files or personal recordings.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide and improve our services:

Data Type Purpose Collection Method
Anonymous Usage Analytics App improvement and crash reporting Umeng SDK
Subscription Information Purchase verification and subscription management Apple StoreKit
Advertising Identifiers Personalized advertisements (optional) Google AdMob

2. Audio Content Privacy

Local Processing Only:

  • All audio recording, editing, and processing occurs locally on your device
  • Your audio files never leave your device unless you explicitly share them
  • We do not have access to your recordings or edited audio content
  • No audio data is transmitted to our servers or third parties

3. How We Use Information

The limited information we collect is used for:

  • Service Improvement: Understanding app usage patterns to enhance features
  • Analytics: Anonymous crash reports and performance metrics
  • Advertising: Displaying relevant ads (can be disabled with subscription)
  • Subscription Management: Processing and verifying in-app purchases

We do not sell personal information to third parties.

4. Permissions Required

The app requests the following permissions:

  • Microphone Access: Required for audio recording functionality
  • Photo Library Access: Optional, for importing audio files from your device
  • File System Access: For saving and managing your audio projects

You can manage these permissions through your device's Settings app at any time.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Local processing ensures your audio content remains on your device
  • No collection of personally identifiable information (PII)
  • Secure transmission of anonymous analytics data
  • Compliance with Apple's App Store privacy guidelines
  • Regular security updates and improvements

6. Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following third-party services:

  • Apple StoreKit: For in-app purchase processing (Apple's privacy policy applies)
  • Google AdMob: For advertising services (Google's privacy policy applies)
  • Umeng Analytics: For anonymous usage analytics (Umeng's privacy policy applies)

These services have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. International Users

This app is available worldwide. We comply with applicable privacy laws including:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for EU users
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) for California residents
  • Other applicable local privacy regulations

9. Data Retention

Since we don't collect your audio content:

  • Your audio files remain on your device until you delete them
  • Anonymous analytics data is retained for service improvement purposes
  • Subscription information is managed by Apple and subject to their retention policies

10. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access information about what data we collect
  • Request deletion of your data (where applicable)
  • Opt-out of analytics collection
  • Disable advertising personalization
  • Manage app permissions through your device settings

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app or through other appropriate communication channels.
