Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2024
Privacy First: Petal Browser is designed with privacy as a core principle. We believe your browsing data should remain private and under your control.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Petal Browser. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile browser application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

Key Privacy Principles:

  • Minimal data collection
  • Local data storage whenever possible
  • No tracking or profiling
  • User control over data
  • Transparency in our practices

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information We DO NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Browsing history or visited websites
  • Search queries or terms
  • Downloaded files or content
  • Bookmarks or saved passwords
  • Location data
  • Device contacts or photos

2.2 Technical Information We May Collect

To improve app performance and fix bugs, we may collect limited technical information:

Data Type Purpose Storage
App crash reports Bug fixing and stability improvement Anonymous, temporary
App usage statistics Feature optimization Aggregated, no personal data
Device type and OS version Compatibility testing Anonymous

3. How We Use Information

Any information we collect is used solely for:

  • App Improvement: Fixing bugs and enhancing performance
  • Feature Development: Understanding which features are most useful
  • Security: Protecting against malicious activities
  • Support: Helping users with technical issues

We never use your information for:

  • Advertising or marketing
  • Selling to third parties
  • Creating user profiles
  • Tracking across websites

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

Most of your data is stored locally on your device:

  • Browsing History: Stored locally, never transmitted
  • Bookmarks: Saved on your device only
  • Settings and Preferences: Local storage
  • Downloaded Files: Remain on your device

4.2 Security Measures

We implement multiple security measures:

  • End-to-end encryption for any data transmission
  • Secure HTTPS connections for all web requests
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • No server-side storage of personal data
  • Built-in protection against malicious websites

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Search Engines

When you use search engines through our app:

  • Your queries are sent directly to the search engine you choose
  • We do not intercept or store search terms
  • Each search engine has its own privacy policy
  • You can change your default search engine at any time

5.2 Websites You Visit

When browsing websites:

  • Websites may collect their own data according to their privacy policies
  • We provide built-in ad and tracker blocking
  • You can enable additional privacy protections in settings
  • Private browsing mode provides extra protection

5.3 App Store Analytics

App stores (Apple App Store, Google Play) may collect:

  • Download and update statistics
  • App performance metrics
  • Crash reports (anonymous)

6. Private Browsing Mode

Enhanced Privacy: Our private browsing mode provides additional protection:

  • No browsing history saved
  • Cookies and site data deleted after session
  • Enhanced tracker blocking
  • No form data or search suggestions saved
  • Automatic HTTPS upgrades when available

7. Your Data Rights and Controls

7.1 Data Control

You have full control over your data:

  • Clear Data: Delete browsing history, cookies, and cache anytime
  • Export Data: Export bookmarks and settings
  • Privacy Settings: Customize privacy and security options
  • Ad Blocking: Enable/disable ad and tracker blocking

7.2 Data Deletion

To delete your data:

  • Use the "Clear Browsing Data" option in settings
  • Uninstall the app to remove all local data
  • Contact us for any questions about data deletion

8. Children's Privacy

We are committed to protecting children's privacy:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • Parents can use parental controls to restrict access
  • Safe browsing features help protect young users
  • If we learn we have collected a child's personal information, we will delete it immediately

9. International Data Transfers

Since we minimize data collection:

  • Most data remains on your local device
  • Any technical data collected is processed according to applicable privacy laws
  • We comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulations
  • Data transfers are secured and limited to essential functions only

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an in-app notification for significant changes
  • Posting updates on our website

Your continued use of the app after changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Legal Compliance

11.1 Law Enforcement

We may disclose information if required by law, but since we collect minimal data:

  • We have very limited information to provide
  • Most user data is stored locally and not accessible to us
  • We will notify users of requests when legally permitted

11.2 Applicable Laws

This Privacy Policy is governed by applicable privacy laws, including:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
  • Local privacy laws where applicable
