Welcome to "IDPhoto". We value your privacy and are committed to handling your data in a transparent and secure manner.

1. On-device Processing

All image processing (including background removal, beautification, and replacement) is performed on your device. We do not upload, store, or share any personal portrait data without your consent.

2. Information We May Collect

  • Basic device information: device model and OS version for compatibility and performance optimization.
  • Anonymous usage statistics: used to improve user experience (e.g., feature usage counts, crash logs). No personally identifiable information is collected.

3. Third-party Services

We may integrate third-party services (e.g., Google AdMob, Umeng Analytics) to provide ads and analytics. These services may collect anonymous data; please refer to their privacy policies.

4. Data Security

We adopt reasonable technical and managerial measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Your Rights

  • Access and correction: you can view or adjust relevant settings within the app.
  • Withdraw consent: if you wish to opt-out of anonymous analytics, you can disable related options in settings (if available).

6. Children’s Privacy

If you are a minor, please use this app under the guidance of your guardian.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this policy from time to time due to business or regulatory changes. Major changes will be notified in-app.
