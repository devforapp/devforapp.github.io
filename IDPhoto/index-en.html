Key Features

AI Background Removal

Powered by Apple Vision framework, precisely detects portrait and hair details for one-tap background removal.

Multiple Size Presets

Built-in 1-inch/2-inch and common standards for exams, teaching certifications, and US/Japan/Schengen visas.

Background Color Switch

One-tap red/white/blue backgrounds, with gray, gradient and custom colors supported.

Smart Capture & Quality Check

Real-time face alignment, posture correction, lighting detection; auto evaluate sharpness/brightness/blink and prompt retake.
