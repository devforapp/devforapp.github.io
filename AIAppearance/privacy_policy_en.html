Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 1, 2024

Important Notice: Beauty Scan is an entertainment app. Analysis results are for entertainment purposes only and do not represent any scientific or medical advice.

1. Information Collection

Beauty Scan respects your privacy. The information we collect includes:

  • Facial Images: Photos you upload are used solely for facial analysis and are not stored on our servers
  • Analysis Results: Beauty scores and facial feature analysis results may be temporarily cached to improve user experience
  • Usage Statistics: Anonymous app usage data such as feature usage frequency and app launch counts
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and other technical information to optimize app performance

2. Facial Analysis Processing

Photos you upload are sent through encrypted connections to Microsoft Azure Face API for facial analysis. Photos are immediately deleted after analysis completion and are not stored. We do not retain your facial images or biometric data.

3. Data Usage

We use collected information to:

  • Provide facial analysis and beauty scoring services
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Provide customer support services
  • Send important app update notifications

4. Advertising Services

This app integrates Google AdMob advertising services. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for personalized ad delivery. You can choose to limit ad tracking in your device settings.

5. Subscription Information

If you purchase a Pro subscription, subscription management is handled by Apple's App Store. We can only access subscription status information and do not obtain your payment information. Subscriptions will auto-renew, and you can cancel anytime in App Store settings.

6. Data Analytics

We use UMeng Analytics to collect anonymous usage data, including app launches, feature usage, and crash reports. This data helps us improve app quality and user experience.

7. Local Storage

The app may store the following information on your device:

  • Language preference settings
  • Daily free test count records
  • Pro subscription status
  • App settings and preferences

8. Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party services:

  • Microsoft Azure Face API: Provides facial analysis services
  • Google AdMob: Provides advertising services
  • UMeng Analytics: Provides data analytics services

When using these services, your data will be subject to the privacy policies of the respective service providers.

9. Data Security

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encrypted transmission, secure storage, and access controls. However, please note that no method of network transmission or storage is 100% secure.

10. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you discover that we have collected personal information from a child, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.

11. Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated through in-app notifications or other appropriate means. Continued use of the app indicates your acceptance of the updated privacy policy.
