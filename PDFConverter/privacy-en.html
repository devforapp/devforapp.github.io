Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2025-11-10

1. On-Device Processing Principle

PDFConverter performs conversions, merge/split, and compression primarily on your device. Unless you explicitly choose to share or use cloud-related features, we do not upload or remotely store your documents.

2. Information We May Collect

  • Basic device information (model, OS version) for compatibility and performance optimization.
  • Anonymous usage and diagnostics (e.g., feature usage counts, crash logs) to improve the product. No personally identifiable information is collected.
  • Permissions: to select and save files, the app may request access to Files/Photos/Library; only when relevant features are used.

3. Document Handling and Permissions

  • Document sources: Files app, Photos, or other sources you explicitly authorize.
  • Local processing: processed outputs (e.g., converted PDFs) are stored locally or to a destination you choose.
  • Sharing and export: when you actively share or export, documents are sent to the target app/service per your instruction and subject to that service’s privacy policy.

4. Third-Party Services

We may integrate third-party analytics (e.g., Umeng Analytics) to provide anonymized usage statistics. Such services may process non-identifiable device and usage data; please refer to their official privacy policies for details.

5. Data Security

We employ reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including data minimization, on-device processing, and encrypted transmission where applicable. No system can guarantee absolute security; you acknowledge and accept residual risks.

6. Your Rights

  • Access and correction: review and adjust relevant permissions/settings in-app or in system settings.
  • Withdraw consent: opt-out of anonymous analytics in settings (if available).
  • Data control: you can delete documents or cache generated by the app at any time.

7. Children’s Privacy

If you are a minor, please use this app under the guidance of your guardian.

8. Cross-Border Transfers

If you choose third-party cloud services for transfer/backup, related data may be processed on servers located outside your country and governed by third-party policies.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this policy due to feature or regulatory changes. Material changes will be notified in-app or on the website. Your continued use indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
