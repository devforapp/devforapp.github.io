Welcome to PicJointer. We value your privacy and are committed to handling your information in a transparent and controlled manner.

1. On‑Device Processing

Image processing (collage layouts, filters and adjustments) is performed entirely on your device. We do not upload, store, or share any photos or biometric data without your consent.

2. Information We May Collect

  • Basic device information (e.g., model, OS version) for compatibility and performance optimization.
  • Anonymous usage analytics to improve product experience (e.g., feature usage, crash logs). No personally identifiable information is collected.

3. Third‑Party Services

We may integrate third‑party services for ads and basic analytics (e.g., Google AdMob, Umeng). These services may collect anonymous data to provide analysis and operational support. Please refer to their official privacy policies.

4. Subscriptions & Payments

Pro subscriptions and one‑time unlocks are processed by the platform’s in‑app purchase system (Apple App Store / Google Play). Trials and renewal rules follow the in‑app display and platform terms.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and administrative measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction.

6. Your Rights

  • Access & Correction: You can view and adjust relevant settings in the app.
  • Withdraw Consent: You can disable anonymous analytics in settings (if available).

7. Children’s Privacy

If you are a minor, please use the app under the guidance of a guardian.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this policy as business or regulations change. Significant changes will be notified via in‑app announcements or dialogs.
