Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information Collection

We collect the following types of information:

  • Location information: Used for adding location watermarks to photos
  • Photos and images: Used for watermarking and verification
  • Device information: Used to improve app performance

All information is stored and processed locally on your device.

2. Information Usage

We use the collected information for:

  • Adding time and location watermarks to photos
  • Verifying image authenticity
  • Improving app functionality and user experience
  • Providing customer support

3. Information Protection

We take the following measures to protect your information:

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • Image verification is performed locally, not uploaded to servers
  • Industry-standard encryption technology
  • Regular security updates

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store: For app distribution and subscription management
  • Apple Maps: For location services

These services have their own privacy policies.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Delete the app and all data
  • Deny location access permissions
  • Cancel subscription at any time

6. Contact Us

If you have any privacy questions or concerns, please contact us through the in-app feedback feature.
