Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 25, 2025

1. Information Collection

AI Translator respects your privacy. We do not collect, store, or share your personal translation content. All translations are processed locally on your device or sent through encrypted connections to Google ML Kit services.

2. Translation Data

Text you input for translation is only used to provide translation services. For online translation, text is sent to Google ML Kit API for processing but is not stored by us. Offline translation is performed entirely on your local device without transmitting any data.

3. Usage Data

We may collect anonymous usage statistics such as app launches and feature usage frequency to improve the app experience. This data does not include any personally identifiable information or translation content.

4. Subscription Information

If you purchase a Premium subscription, subscription management is handled by Apple's App Store. We only have access to subscription status information and do not obtain your payment information.

5. Local Storage

The app may store the following information on your device: language preference settings, translation history (if you enable this feature), downloaded offline language packs. You can clear this data anytime in app settings.

6. Third-Party Services

This app uses Google ML Kit to provide translation services. When using online translation, your translation requests are subject to Google's privacy policy. We recommend reading Google's privacy policy for details.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about the privacy policy, please contact us at support@aitranslator.app.
